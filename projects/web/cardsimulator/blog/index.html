Student Project: Card Picker

By Jim (a.k.a. YL)

I made something called card picker. There are 52 cards and when you click a card the card flips and reveals a random 52 deck

I learned many things when making this card picker. One of the new thing I learned was how to make an animation. That was very interesting.

If I still had time I would've added an image to the cards and have more features and buttons in the card picker.
